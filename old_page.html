TERMS AND CONDITIONS

By submitting this Learning and Development Nomination Form, the parties agree to the following conditions regarding attendance and substitution:

  1. ADVANCE NOTICE OF CANCELLATION. The Primary Participant shall notify the appropriate office at least three (3) days prior to the training if he/she will not pursue the scheduled Learning & Development Intervention (LDI).
  2. FORMAL JUSTIFICATION AND SUBSTITUTION. The Primary Participant must provide a formal written justification for declining the training. Upon this formal notification, the Alternate/Substitute Participant (ASP) will be endorsed to attend the training.
  3. SAME-DAY CANCELLATION. In cases in which the Primary Participant declines the training on the actual day of the event, the cancellation shall only be accepted if it is due to a major reason.
  4. ACCEPTABLE MAJOR REASONS. Valid major reasons for same-day cancellation and the immediate endorsement of the Alternate/Substitute Participant (ASP) include the following:
    1. Illness.
    2. Force majeure.
    3. Death.
    4. Urgent and critical official business directives from the department.
  5. RELEVANCE TO FUNCTIONS. The nomination to the LDI must be formally justified based on its relevance to the participant’s actual functions and specific development needs. The intervention must purposefully address identified core, leadership, or functional competencies.
  6. APPROVAL AND ENDORSEMENT. The nomination, including the designation of the ASP, is only considered valid and final upon the complete briefing of the nominee and the official signature of the Office/Unit Head.
